## ðŸ’¡ About the Project
This project explores how emotional disorders such as anxiety and social phobia impact high school studentsâ€™ social interactions. To address this, we developed a 2D narrative game designed to promote emotional awareness and empathy among teenagers.

Grounded in academic research and supported by field data from CEFET-MG (Contagem), its story unfolds in a school inspired by CEFET, following students navigating challenges of socialization and belonging, highlighting the obstacles their psychological disorders can create socializing in the school environment.

With 80.8% of surveyed students reporting emotional difficulties in school, the project reinforces the urgency of this discussion and demonstrates how games can serve as meaningful tools for reflection and engagement.
